DRIP KL Present: 19:26

Fri, Jul 17 · 2:00 PM
Drip Kuala Lumpur
<p>DRIP KL and Horizon proudly welcome <strong>19:26</strong> for an exclusive Kuala Lumpur performance on <strong>17 July 2026</strong>.</p><p>A defining force in the new wave of melodic electronic music, 19:26 has built a reputation for blending cinematic emotion, neoclassical influences, and forward-thinking electronic sound design. His music exists in the space between memory and the future, where powerful melodies, hypnotic rhythms, and emotional storytelling converge to create unforgettable dancefloor moments.</p><p>Following acclaimed releases on <strong>Afterlife</strong> and <strong>Siamese</strong>, 19:26 unveiled his most ambitious body of work to date with <em>The Passage EP</em> on Afterlife, a deeply personal exploration of transformation and inner transition. His breakout release <strong>XX</strong> became a defining milestone, reaching top positions on the Beatport charts and earning support across major international radio stations. His collaboration with <strong>Anyma</strong>, <em>Giza</em>, further cemented his place among the most exciting artists in melodic electronic music today.</p><p>Supported by industry heavyweights including Tale Of Us, Anyma, MRAK, Adriatique, Mathame, Argy, Miss Monique, David Guetta, and Ti&euml;sto, 19:26 has captivated audiences worldwide through a sound that balances emotional depth with club-focused precision.</p><p>His performances have taken him to some of the world&#39;s most iconic stages, including Afterlife Festival, Zamna, H&iuml; Ibiza, Space Miami, Kappa Futur Festival, and the legendary Pyramids of Giza, where his set became a viral phenomenon celebrated for its cinematic intensity and emotional impact.</p><p data-end="980" data-start="732">Supporting him on the night are <strong data-end="776" data-start="764">Victor G</strong>, <strong>WESUFO</strong>, and <strong data-end="803" data-start="794">Boriz</strong>, who will set the tone with a carefully curated selection of melodic house, progressive sounds, and peak-time energy, creating the perfect build-up to the headline performance.</p><p data-end="529" data-start="177">For this special showcase, we&#39;re bringing the <strong data-end="665" data-start="636">Sunset by Neon&nbsp;</strong>energy indoors.
